About Us

Welcome to Innovar Centre

With a changing world, the global skills landscape is undergoing a profound transformation. Rapid advancements in technology, particularly in automation and AI, are reshaping job roles; increasing demand for specialized skills in emerging technologies. Innovar is bridging the potential gap between employers’ expectations of skills and job expectations of graduates.

We provide a platform for upskilling, practical coaching, student internships, apprenticeship and bootcamp opportunities that are crucial in making students job-ready and more attractive to potential employers.

Designed for final year students, graduates & professionals in:

⦁ Interior Design & Architecture

⦁ Construction Management

⦁ Property development

⦁ Real estate & Urban Design

⦁ Quantity Surveying

⦁ Civil Engineering
